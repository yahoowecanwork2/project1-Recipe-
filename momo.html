Here's a simple and delicious Veg momos Recipe


Ingredients:


For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water (as needed for kneading)

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped spring onions
  • 1 tsp grated garlic
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 2-3 dry red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
    • Gradually add water and knead to form a smooth, soft dough.
    • Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Preparing the Filling:
    • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger, sauté until fragrant.
    • Add onions and spring onions, sauté until soft
    • Stir in cabbage, carrots, soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables soften but still have a crunch.
    • Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool.
  3. Shaping the Momos:
    • Divide the dough into small balls.
    • Roll each ball into a thin circle (about 3 inches in diameter).
    • Place a spoonful of filling in the center of the circle.
    • Gently fold the edges over the filling, pleating as you go, and seal the top.
  4. Steaming the Momos:
    • Grease a steamer plate with oil.
    • Place the momos on the plate, leaving some space between them.
    • Steam the momos for 10-12 minutes or until they appear translucent and shiny.
  5. Making the Dipping Sauce:
    • Boil tomatoes and red chilies in water for 5-6 minutes.
    • Once cooled, blend the tomatoes, chilies, garlic, salt, vinegar, and a bit of water to a smooth paste.
    • Heat oil in a pan and sauté the paste for 2-3 minutes to enhance the flavors.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve the steamed momos hot with the spicy dipping sauce on the side.
Enjoy your homemade momos!
