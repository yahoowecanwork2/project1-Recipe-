Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla or flatbread
  • 1/4 cup hummus or any spread (like mayonnaise, cream cheese, or pesto)
  • 1/2 cup mixed fresh veggies (lettuce, spinach, cucumber, tomatoes, bell peppers, grated carrots)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or salad dressing (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. the Hummus:
      2. Lay the tortilla or flatbread on a clean surface and spread a thin layer of hummus (or your chosen spread) evenly over it.
  2. the Veggies:
      3. Layer the fresh veggies on top of the hummus, arranging them evenly. Add shredded cheese if you like.
  3. season and Dress:
    • Drizzle a little olive oil or salad dressing over the veggies if you want extra flavor. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Wrap It Up:
    • Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to the top
  5. Wrap it up
    • Slice the wrap in half and enjoy immediately

This veggie wrap is quick to make, super healthy, and customizable based on what you have at home! You can add chicken, turkey, or any protein for extra heartiness.
