Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or sourdough)
  • 2-3 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or any of your choice)
  • 1-2 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter the Bread:
      2. Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each slice of bread. This will make the sandwich golden and crispy when grilled.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich:
    • Place the cheese slices between the two slices of bread, with the buttered sides facing out.
  3. Grill the Sandwich:
    • Heat a skillet over medium heat.
    • Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, pressing gently with a spatula until the bread is golden brown and the cheese has melted.
  4. Serve:
    • Remove from the skillet, slice in half, and serve hot with tomato ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.
Enjoy your simple and delicious grilled cheese sandwich! You can add extras like tomato slices, ham, or avocado for more flavor.k
